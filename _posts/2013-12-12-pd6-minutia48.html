The Price is Right!

  • Last group's presentation
  • Derek, Tyrone, Roger, and Helen created an online version of the TV game show The Price is Right
  • After logging in, you can start a new game:
    • A random product is shown from the Etsy and Edmunds API's.
    • Your job is to guesstimate the product's worth (given 3 guesses).
    • In order to land a correct guess, you have to have <=10% error.
    • Guessing correctly adds the product's worth to your total score.
    • When your game is over, your score is compared to and ranked among other scores within a database.

Bogus Email Addresses

Head to mailinator.com to get your very own temporary email addresses! Want to sign up for one-shot-deals but don't want to use your private email? Don't want to waste time making a whole new email address? Then this is the solution you've been looking for! Mailinator is email stripped down to being receive and read only. One thing to note: don't send private info to this email. It IS a public email address (plus it gets deleted after some time).
